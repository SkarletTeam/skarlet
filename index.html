Skarlet Corp.

Cultivating Community Empowerment




At Skarlet Corp, we provide comprehensive IT and cybersecurity services tailored to meet the needs of small to medium-sized businesses. Our backup solutions, such as Email/Business Suite Backup, NAS Backup, Endpoint Backup, and Server Backup, ensure your critical data is safe and easily recoverable. We also offer specialized services like QuickBooks Backup. We offer full Microsoft 365 (M365) packages, including Azure AD for secure identity management and VPN setups for secure remote access.

Our Remote Monitoring and Management (RMM) tools, paired with Hardware Firewalls, Web Application Firewalls (WAF), and Web Filtering/DNS solutions, ensure that your network stays protected and optimized 24/7. For businesses looking to outsource, we offer NOC Services, including Helpdesk, Ticketing System, and additional support services.

To keep your business secure, we provide a full Cybersecurity Suite of services: Antivirus, Password Manager, Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA) and Single Sign-On (SSO), Security Awareness Training, Managed Detection and Response (MDR), Dark Web Monitoring, and Vulnerability Scanning services. These offerings ensure your business is protected from every angle, providing peace of mind against evolving cyber threats.

Privacy Policy

At Skarlet Corp., we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines the types of information we collect and how it is used.

Information We Collect

We may collect personal information such as your name, email address, and any other relevant details when you:

  • Schedule a meeting with us
  • Request downloads or resources
  • Sign up for our newsletter or marketing communications

How We Use Your Information

The information we collect will be used solely for the following purposes:

  • To schedule and confirm your meeting
  • To send you requested downloads or resources
  • To send you marketing materials (if you have opted in)

Data Sharing and Security

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties, except as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined above (e.g., sending requested downloads via an email service provider). We take reasonable steps to protect your personal information from unauthorized access or disclosure.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, or request the deletion of your personal information at any time. To exercise these rights, please contact us at info@skarlet.org.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
